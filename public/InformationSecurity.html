Designing a Network Architecture solution

Information Security Management within organisations

This report presents a comprehensive security and compliance strategy tailored for BFS, a supermarket chain in the UK. It outlines the design of a secure, scalable network architecture across multiple sites implementing VPN connectivity, intrusion prevention, robust access controls, and encrypted storage solutions.

Compliance with industry standards such as PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard), UK GDPR, and CIS Critical Security Controls (Cybersecurity best practices) is emphasized to ensure the protection of sensitive customer data and adherence to legal requirements. The report also includes a detailed risk assessment, identifying potential threats and vulnerabilities, and proposes mitigation strategies to enhance the overall security posture of BFS.
